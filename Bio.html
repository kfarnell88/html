My Bio


Kelsey Farnell is a graphic designer born and raised in Tampa, Florida. She received her bachelor's degree in advertising from the University of South Florida (go Bulls!) and also holds a bachelors in criminology. Her career began in newspaper advertising and direct mail for the financial industry, but has since grown to all fields of graphic design, including web design and branding.

Kelsey is a member of Ad 2 Tampa Bay and served as Ad 2's Co-Creative Director of Public Service (2013-2014), where she had the opportunity to art direct a nationally recognized 1st place public service campaign.

Her motto is: 'Give the people what they want.'
